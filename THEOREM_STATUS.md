# Shen_work theorem status

This file is the source of truth for whether a paper theorem has actually been
formalized.  The current invariant is:

- `rg -n "\bsorry\b|axiom|admit" ShenWork --glob '*.lean'` should return no Lean
  proof holes.
- `0 sorry` does not mean the paper main theorems are proved.
- Paper-level theorems must not be represented by toy witnesses, constant
  solutions that ignore initial data, or placeholder PDE predicates.

## Status labels

- `not stated`: no Lean theorem currently claims this paper theorem.
- `statement target`: theorem statement is represented as a Lean `Prop` target,
  but is not yet proved.
- `accurately stated`: theorem statement matches the intended mathematical
  theorem and is ready to prove.
- `proved`: theorem is proved from non-toy definitions.

## Externalized statement targets

The repository still contains many paper-level theorem targets encoded as
`def ... : Prop := ...`.  Projection/accessor lemmas from such a target are
useful interfaces, but they are not mathematical proofs of the paper result
unless there is a theorem such as `Foo_proved : Foo` proved from lower-level
definitions.

Current scan of theorem-like paper targets in `Paper1/Statements.lean`,
`Paper2/Statements.lean`, and `Paper3/Statements.lean`:

- `proved`: 15 targets/branches have same-name `_proved` theorem/lemma.
- `statement target`: 64 theorem-like targets remain without same-name
  `_proved` theorems; their remaining analytic work is visible as open targets,
  explicit package fields, or prior theorem assumptions rather than hidden
  theorem-shaped projection wrappers.

This does not mean the paper proofs are complete.  `_proved` is reserved for
direct branch proofs, corrected targets, or explicit bridge theorems rather
than raw package field access.  Do not count projection lemmas of the form
`(h : Foo) -> ...` as proofs of `Foo`, and do not treat package fields as
discharged analytic work.  Paper1's former all-in-one analytic-data package,
Paper2's former all-in-one analytic package, and the Paper3 package-field
projection wrappers have been removed from Lean sources.  Paper2 Lemmas
2.1--2.4 are still externalized in `SemigroupEstimateData` for the full
semigroup estimates, but their zero-output/zero-embedding branches are now
proved directly; Paper3 Lemmas A.2--A.5 are definitional aliases/accessors for
those same Paper2 targets and expose the same direct zero-output branches.
Paper2 analytic targets are open except for direct branches/bridges and formal
obstruction theorems; Paper3 compactness/stability/persistence/comparison targets remain
externalized in `StabilityNorms`, `CompactnessData`, and `Paper3Constants`.
These packages make the assumptions explicit but do not yet instantiate the analytic semigroup,
bounded-domain PDE estimates, stability, compactness, constants, or resolvent
theory.
