Remark 1.1 (Local existence). This remark is about the local existence of classical solutions of (1.9).
(1) As mentioned before, most studies in the literature on the existence of classical solutions

(2) Proposition 1.1 includes the existence of classical solutions of (1.9) with m < 1. When
m < 1, вҲҮum (t, x) may fail to exist at points (t, x) where u(t, x) = 0 for a classical solution
