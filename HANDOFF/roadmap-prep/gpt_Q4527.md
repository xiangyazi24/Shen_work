# Important non-circularity / non-vacuity qualification

The restart construction fixes the false `S_N(0)` trace, but it does **not** make the restart initial inequality automatic. One must genuinely prove

```lean
∀ x ∈ Set.Icc (0 : ℝ) 1,
  squareHeatBarrier M f τ x ≤ u τ x.
```

That is the honest comparison seed at positive time. In the committed paper-positive route it follows from the half-floor estimate and is packaged by

```lean
squareHeatBarrier_paperPositiveConstSeed_initial_le.
```

In a floor-free Jensen route, the corresponding positive-time `hinitial` must come from the chosen restart seed/near-zero trace estimate. But there is no longer any logical contradiction: for `τ > 0`, `S_N(τ)f` is the genuine positive heat profile, and the strip calculus, initial comparison, and strict barrier positivity are mutually compatible.
