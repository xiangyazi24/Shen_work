## What it consumes

The reusable lemma should consume only:

1. the terminal time hypotheses (0 < t, and t ≤ T or t < T);

1. continuity of the two terminal slices, or directly continuity of the difference/test;

1. a Lipschitz/AC certificate hGu for the terminal difference/test on Icc 0 1;

1. nonnegativity of the Lipschitz constant if the structure stores one.

It should not consume:

```javascript

hdecay
hzero
hsrcB
HSpectral
strict positivity
matched-divergence PDE identities
source-time-C¹
```

Those are higher-level producer obligations. This lemma is only the terminal-test adapter.
