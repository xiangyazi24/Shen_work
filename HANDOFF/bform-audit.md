Scope: `BFormBankedInputs` in `ShenWork/Paper2/IntervalBFormDirectClassical.lean:73-112` and `PositiveDatumBFormLocalComponents` in `ShenWork/Paper2/IntervalBFormPositiveDatumLocalExistence.lean:30-68`. The first structure has 10 Lean fields because `MInit` and `haInit` are separate fields.

Search method: I grepped field names and target types across `ShenWork/**/*.lean`, then inspected the candidate definitions/theorems. The producer files cited below were also grepped for `sorry`, `admit`, and `axiom`; the only hit was a prose comment in `IntervalBankChemSliceFix.lean:59`, not proof code.

Status key: `PRODUCED` means a cited producer has no extra field-level hypotheses beyond its explicit datum/package input. `PARTIAL` means a cited producer/constructor exists but carries further sub-hypotheses. `CARRIED` means I found no repo producer for the exact target field; the table names the missing field/lemma.
