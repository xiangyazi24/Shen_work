## Soundness notes

1. **No PID/PPID coercion is needed.**  The new frontier and `ChiNegDatumUniformConstructionStrong` both quantify over `PaperPositiveInitialDatum`; the theorem does not try to fill the weak PID factory.

2. **This is weaker in datum coverage than the weak faithful frontier but exactly aligned with the paper headline path.**  It only asks for PPID data, which is the datum class consumed by `StrongPath.chiNeg_theorem_1_1_of_strong`.

3. **The lifespan reorder is identical to the weak faithful route.**  The returned `δ` from `exists_uniform_EWA_lifespan` is independent of the datum class.  The only change is the hypothesis passed to the frontier.

4. **It remains conditional on the strong faithful realization frontier.**  This file does not discharge the analytic EWA realization atoms or the coupled reduced classical core.  It merely gives the right PPID-typed carrier and headline bridge.

5. **Do not route through `chiNeg_theorem_1_1_faithful`.**  That theorem consumes the weak `ChiNegDatumUniformConstructionFaithful`; the purpose here is to use `StrongPath.chiNeg_theorem_1_1_of_strong` instead.
