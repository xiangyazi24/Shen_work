The closed-window capstone is:



## 5. Anti-circularity checklist

Do **not** implement any of the following:

```text
- prove X continuity by differentiating the PDE in time;
- prove X continuity by applying an unbounded spatial derivative operator
  to the already continuous W trajectory;
- assume pointwise/BUC derivative continuity plus a uniform L² bound implies
  strong L² continuity (mass may escape to +∞);
- prove F continuity from F_t or from a mild equation for F;
- differentiate F in space inside the X Duhamel history;
- use hFcont to prove the first W continuity, then derive hFcont from W;
- ask for gradient continuity at the same restart face where the initial datum
  is known only in L²;
- reuse an arbitrary preliminary Hilbert forcing as the final physical F
  without the a.e. representative theorem.
```

The committed route avoids all of these.
