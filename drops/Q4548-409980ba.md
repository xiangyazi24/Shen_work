The exact definitional equality is

```lean

  = coupledChemDivSourceCoeffs p u s n
```

not

```lean

  = bFormSourceCoeffs p u s n.
```

# 5. Zero-mode sanity check

Under the flux regularity and endpoint-vanishing hypotheses used by `divMode_of_sliceC1_open`, taking `n = 0` gives

```text
coupledChemDivSourceCoeffs p u s 0 = 0,
```

because the integral of a spatial divergence is the boundary flux, and the Neumann chemotaxis flux vanishes at both endpoints. Hence

```text
bFormSourceCoeffs p u s 0
  = coupledLogisticSourceCoeffs p u s 0.
```

This is another immediate check that `coupledChemDivSourceCoeffs` cannot equal `bFormSourceCoeffs` in general.
