realized EWA fixed point plus reduced classical core, no old logistic-only `hfp`.

of the EWA realization route; it bypasses it.  So the answer depends on route goal:

- If the goal is the **weakest abstract theorem hypothesis**, use `ChiNegDatumUniformCore`.
- If the goal is the **faithful EWA realization route**, Task279's
  `ChiNegStrongFaithfulRealizationFrontier` is the current narrow live residual.

strict-negative branch.
