## Why this theorem is the best next patch

It reduces a real mismatch introduced by the new `On` consumer: the bank stores `DuhamelSourceTimeC1On`, but the only current `hB_global` producer still wants global `DuhamelSourceTimeC1`.

It also removes another latent false assumption: positive paper data should provide `Continuous u₀`, not `Continuous (intervalDomainLift u₀)`.

This is smaller than proving the missing logistic or chem-div source time-C¹ packages from scratch, and it unlocks honest use of the bank’s `hsrcBDirect` field.
