# Q3836 — prioritized conditional-hypothesis assessment for Papers 1–3

## Executive summary

The three papers are in very different states.

* **Paper 2** is the closest to a real unconditional interval-domain theorem.  Its remaining gaps are technical but local and repo-native: positive-time source/spectral bootstrap for the same mild solution, plus uniform-core positivity/nonnegativity if not already packaged.  I would treat these as **high-priority dischargeable**, not as open PDE research.
* **Paper 1** is partly close, but the main remaining floors are more serious: the per-step whole-line Green/flux residual and a Schauder fixed-point principle on the trap.  Some carried fields are already reducible to trap/profile lemmas, but `hprinciple` and the per-step floor are real work.
* **Paper 3** is mostly statement-layer and bridge-layer complete, but its major floors are intentionally the actual bounded-domain PDE program: global existence/boundedness, persistence, compactness, sectorial/nonlinear stability, and Lyapunov convergence.  The repo has many formula/spectral and interval wrapper wins, but full Paper 3 remains a longer PDE-library build.

Below “already dischargeable” means either the repo already has a producer or the remaining theorem is mostly glue around committed infrastructure. “Deep” means it requires new PDE/functional-analysis infrastructure, not a repackaging.
