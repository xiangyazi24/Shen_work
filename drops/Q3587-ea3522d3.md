But it removes the separate mild-local/frontier input from the final wiring theorem, because `hQuant` is already a stronger local-existence source.  That means the downstream headline theorem can be stated from fewer residual hypotheses.

* Adding `UniformContinuation.paper2_theorem_1_1_of_hUniform_and_mildLocal` that drops the unused `hlocal` argument is also **interface cleanup**, although worthwhile.
* The `FinalWiring` `hQuant` cleanup is the smallest patch I see that actually reduces the public theorem’s assumption set.

          InitialTrace intervalDomain u₀ u := by
