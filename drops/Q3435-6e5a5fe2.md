## Short answer to the audit question

There is no existing no-local parametric/spectral initial route in `IntervalChiNegH1BridgeInitialWiring.lean`. The no-local core exists one layer lower in `IntervalChiNegH1InitialWiring.lean`; the route-specific wrappers simply have not been propagated. Adding the sixteen `_before` wrappers above is the smallest honest fix. It is implementation-worthy now because it is pure record/wrapper plumbing and removes a misleading manual `hlocal` carry from the active route-facing API.
