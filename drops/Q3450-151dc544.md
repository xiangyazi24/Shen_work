## Why this is the right next task

The current bridge already consumes `hTime`, `hChem`, `hReact`, and `hEqInterior`. The easiest piece to shrink is not `hTime` or `hEqInterior`; those are real parabolic/initial-compatibility inputs. The easy shrink is proving `hReact` and `hChem` from primitive zero-start continuity fields.

After that wrapper lands, a construction-specific producer can target the primitive record above. That keeps the active work in physical-RHS/transfer files and avoids the finite sigma-ladder files.
