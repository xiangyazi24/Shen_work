## Verdict

Yes: the candidate `IntervalChiNegH1PhysicalScalarContinuity.lean` is the right next minimal non-circular step **if it is kept as a pure continuity reducer**. It should not try to prove the hard physical joint-continuity of all formulas from the classical solution in the same task.

The smallest useful version is:

1. prove that strict-slab joint continuity of each scalar integrand gives strict-window continuity of the corresponding Task84 scalar integral;
2. package those three scalar continuities with an existing strict lap-continuity input into Task88's

```lean
ShenWork.Paper2.IntervalChiNegH1PhysicalInitialRHS.H1PhysicalRHSComponentsContinuousStrictBefore p u v T
```

This is honest because Task88 deliberately routes through strict-positive-time component continuity plus an initial-window RHS majorant, not zero-start component continuity. It also avoids any claim that zero-start `lapL2sq` continuity follows from the current classical API.

One refinement: the literal integrand-continuity version

```lean

  (Function.uncurry
    (fun τ x => liftDeriv2 u τ x * H1PhysicalChemTaxisPart p u v τ x))
  (Set.Icc a b ×ˢ Set.Icc (0 : ℝ) 1)
```

is acceptable as a pure wrapper, but it may be too strong for the physical route because it asks for closed-spatial endpoint continuity of literal `liftDeriv2 u`. The better companion wrapper should accept a continuous representative `F` for `liftDeriv2 u` plus interior equality on `Set.Ioo (0 : ℝ) 1`, exactly like the existing lap-component reducers do.

## Circularity and vacuity risks

* Do **not** target `H1PhysicalRHSRouteBefore` yet: it requires closed-window component continuity and therefore zero-start `lapL2sq` continuity.
* Do **not** infer zero-start `lapL2sq` continuity from `IsPaper2ClassicalSolution`, `H1UxxL1ContBefore`, or the Task88 initial RHS majorant. That would smuggle the missing H²/lap trace.
* Do **not** use `H1IdentityRHSIntegrableBefore` to prove the initial RHS majorant and then feed it back into Task88; that is circular. Task87/88 intentionally want an independently produced initial-window RHS majorant.
* Do **not** prove scalar continuity from final bounded-before or H¹ boundedness. That would put estimates after the theorem that consumes them.
* Do **not** hide the scalar functions existentially. Task84 fixed concrete global scalar functions precisely to avoid the degenerate existential triple issue.
* Be cautious with the literal-integrand version: it is honest as a wrapper, but it may be too strong for physical production. The representative version is the safer long-term API.
