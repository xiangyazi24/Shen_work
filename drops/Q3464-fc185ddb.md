## Routes to avoid

1. **Do not target `H1PhysicalRHSRouteBefore` first.** Its component field is closed-window and includes zero-start `lapL2sq` continuity. That would smuggle the missing H²/lap trace if produced from the current classical API.

2. **Do not use `H1EnergyIdentity_of_classicalSolution_and_uxxL1Cont` as the physical identity producer.** It returns an existential triple using the degenerate witnesses `(0, 0, -val + lapL2sq)`. That proves an identity-shaped existential but does not give the physical scalar functions and cannot support the physical estimates.

3. **Do not use `H1SupBoundSqrtDIDataBeforeExists` as the headline physical target.** It hides the scalar functions existentially, so placeholder triples can pass through. Task84 fixed concrete functions; keep them exposed.

4. **Do not prove the square-root estimates from the final bounded-before/H¹ conclusion.** That is circular. The estimates must come from the physical L∞/resolver bounds and Cauchy-Schwarz/Young before the differential inequality is used.

5. **Do not infer continuity of `H1PhysicalTaxisX`, `H1PhysicalUvxxX`, or `H1PhysicalReactX` from `H1UxxL1ContBefore` alone.** L¹ continuity of `u_xx` is not the same as joint continuity of the product integrands or `lapL2sq`.

## Why not identity or sqrt estimates first?

The pointwise H¹ identity/substitution is important, but it is a larger analytic target. The safe entry point is already Task84’s

```lean
H1PhysicalRHSIdentityBefore_of_parametric_and_substitution
```

which requires `hpar` and `hsub`. Proving `hpar` ultimately uses the mixed gradient-time derivative frontier in `IntervalChiNegH1EnergyCore`; proving `hsub` is a real PDE/IBP substitution with the concrete Task84 scalar definitions. That is not a one-line wrapper.

The square-root estimates are also real analysis. They should be proved from the physical L∞ and resolver bounds, not from the final H¹ boundedness theorem. Proving them first is possible only if the needed L∞/resolver estimate frontiers are already available in the exact scalar form; otherwise it risks circularity.

Strict component continuity is the best next producer because it is mechanically reducible from strict-slab joint continuity of the integrands, does not require zero-start H² data, and directly feeds the strict route.
