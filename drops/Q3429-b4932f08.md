
### B. Physical sqrt-bound estimates for `taxisX` / `uvxx` / `reactX`

**Status:** not currently closable from the general APIs without real analytic estimates.

The relevant target is exactly the existing record:

```lean
H1SqrtTermBoundsBefore p u T V₁ V₂ M L taxisX uvxx reactX
```

Current repo data do not fix canonical `taxisX`, `uvxx`, `reactX` functions globally; the route packages carry them together with `hsub`. The estimates also require nontrivial inputs: Cauchy–Schwarz/IBP forms of the split terms, uniform-in-time bounds for `v_x/(1+v)^β` and `v_xx/(1+v)^β`, a bound on the reaction coefficient, and in the current sup-bound shape an `M` controlling the `u` factor. That `M` is dangerously close to the bounded-before conclusion unless supplied by an independent box/local argument.

**Missing assumptions:** explicit physical split definitions plus the actual pointwise/sup estimates. Some resolver boundedness lemmas exist for fixed-time quantities, but the uniform constants needed by `H1SqrtTermBoundsBefore` are not packaged here as pre-bounded-before data.
