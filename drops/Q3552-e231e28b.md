## False/circular routes to avoid

Do not claim any of these as producers of `H1ZeroStartPhysicalPDEInitialCompatibilityBefore`:

* `IsPaper2ClassicalSolution` alone: PDE and strongest continuity fields are open at `t = 0`.
* `IsPaper2GlobalClassicalSolution`: it gives classical solutions on every positive horizon, but the local theorem still uses strict `0 < t`; it does not add the endpoint PDE.
* `P3MoserPDECombinedInitialProducer` or global PDE initial data: these produce scalar initial-window integrability/FTC data and intentionally ignore endpoint values via `Ioc 0 b`; they do not give pointwise `t=0` PDE equality.
* `H1ZeroStartPhysicalRHSDataBefore` / `H1LiftDeriv2ZeroSlabRepresentativeBefore`: these are downstream consumers of the seam, not source producers.
* Any H¹ bounded-before, `H1SupBoundSqrtRHSIntegrableBefore`, sqrt-bound, or H¹ route output: that would be circular and out of scope.
