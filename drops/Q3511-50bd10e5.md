## 4. Namespace hazards

The likely compilation failures are namespace-resolution failures:

- `H1PhysicalRHSStrictInitialRouteBefore_of_classical_componentSquareZero` is in `ShenWork.Paper2.IntervalChiNegH1PhysicalClassicalContinuity`.
- `H1PhysicalRHSComponentSquareSpatialYoungDataBefore` and `H1PhysicalRHSComponentSquareZeroDataBefore_of_spatialYoungData` are in `ShenWork.Paper2.IntervalChiNegH1PhysicalYoungSpatial`.
- `H1PhysicalRHSIdentityBefore`, `H1PhysicalRHSSqrtBoundsBefore`, `H1PhysicalTaxisX`, `H1PhysicalUvxxX`, and `H1PhysicalReactX` are in `ShenWork.Paper2.IntervalChiNegH1PhysicalRHSScalars`.
- `H1PhysicalRHSStrictInitialRouteBefore` and `H1SupBoundSqrtRHSIntegrableBefore_of_physicalStrictInitialRoute` are in `ShenWork.Paper2.IntervalChiNegH1PhysicalInitialRHS`.
- `H1InitialEndpointData` is in `ShenWork.Paper2.IntervalChiNegH1InitialContinuity`, not in `IntervalChiNegH1InitialWiring`; opens inside an imported file are not re-exported.

In the bounded-before proof, the explicit named arguments

```lean
(taxisX := H1PhysicalTaxisX params u v)
(uvxx := H1PhysicalUvxxX params u v)
(reactX := H1PhysicalReactX params u)
```

avoid unnecessary metavariable inference fragility.
